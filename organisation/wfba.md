## Organisational description

[inferred: public website — adapt with confirmed facts before use in applications]

Windsor Forest Brass Academy (WFBA) is a brass education and performance non-profit organisation based in Englefield Green. WFBA provides people of all ages and abilities with the opportunity to learn a brass instrument and develop as musicians. The organisation runs multiple ensembles from beginner to experienced level, and is developing an outreach programme to bring brass music education into schools and community settings across North Surrey and Berkshire. WFBA rehearses at Royal Holloway, University of London.

## Case for support

[draft only — verify facts before use]

WFBA exists to widen access to brass music for people who might otherwise never have the opportunity to learn. Through school outreach, a beginner pathway, and community ensembles, WFBA provides first-access music education and a progression route from absolute beginner to confident ensemble player. A credible safeguarding framework and a partnership with Royal Holloway underpin the organisation's delivery. WFBA is seeking funding to launch its Year 4 school programme, expand outreach workshops, and reduce cost barriers for participants.
